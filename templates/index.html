How to Play

Welcome to Mastermind! The goal is to guess the winning sequence by dragging pegs onto the board (starting at the bottom) and comparing feedback between guesses. After filling each slot of the current row you will be able to submit your guess.

After each guess, you will receive feedback in the form of red, white and black pegs in the right-most column - one for each peg slot. Here's what they represent:

Color Represents
1 Piece has the correct color and position
1 Piece has the correct color, but is in the wrong position
1 piece has both incorrect color and position

You can change your sequence by dragging a different color atop your previous choice before submitting. Good luck!
